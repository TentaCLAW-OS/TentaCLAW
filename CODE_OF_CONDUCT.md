# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

TentaCLAW OS is built by a community of people who believe that local AI inference should be accessible to everyone. We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive
- Welcoming newcomers with helpful guidance
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing these standards. Violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from the community.

## Scope

This applies to all TentaCLAW spaces: GitHub, Discord, forums, and any community channels.

## Contact

Report issues via GitHub Issues or email the maintainers directly.
